Australian Home Loan Lending Policy

1. Maximum Loan Tenure

The maximum home loan tenure is 30 years.

2. Customer Age Eligibility

The maximum age at loan maturity should generally not exceed 75 years.

Example:
A customer aged 60 years may generally receive a maximum tenure of 15 years.

3. Income Verification

Applicants must provide acceptable income verification documents.

For salaried applicants:
- Recent payslips
- Employment verification
- Bank statements

For self-employed applicants:
- Business financial statements
- Tax returns
- Business registration documents

4. Credit Assessment

The lender evaluates:

- Credit history
- Existing liabilities
- Income stability
- Living expenses
- Debt serviceability

5. Loan Approval Process

The approval process includes:

Application submission
Document verification
Credit assessment
Property valuation
Conditional approval
Formal approval
Settlement
